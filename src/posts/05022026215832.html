Why there are so many equals signs in the Epstein files?

Apparently it’s something arcane due to mail servers not liking long strings of text:

The artefact we see here is from something called “quoted printable”, or as we used to call it when it was introduced: “Quoted unreadable”.

…the equals sign is used to say “this should really be one single line, but I’ve broken it in two so that the mail server doesn’t get mad at me”.
